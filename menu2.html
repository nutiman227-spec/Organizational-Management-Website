Classical Management Theory

1. Scientific Management (Frederick Taylor)

Scientific Management focuses on improving worker efficiency through scientific study of tasks and proper work methods.

2. Administrative Theory (Henri Fayol)

Administrative Theory focuses on the overall organization. It includes planning, organizing, leading, and controlling.

3. Bureaucratic Theory (Max Weber)

Bureaucratic Theory emphasizes clear hierarchy, rules, division of labor, and formal procedures.
